each of the possible actions of player 1, namely \emph{rock}, \emph{paper} and
\emph{scissor}.

thus we obtain the following transition probability function (with actions in
the order $rock, paper, scissor$)
\[
	Steps =
	\begin{bmatrix}
		\pi_r & \pi_s & \pi_p & 0     & 0     \\
		\pi_p & \pi_r & \pi_s & 0     & 0     \\
		\pi_s & \pi_p & \pi_r & 0     & 0     \\
		\hline
		\pi_p & \pi_r & 0     & \pi_s & 0     \\
		\pi_s & \pi_p & 0     & \pi_r & 0     \\
		\pi_r & \pi_s & 0     & \pi_p & 0     \\
		\hline
		\pi_s & 0     & \pi_r & 0     & \pi_p \\
		\pi_r & 0     & \pi_p & 0     & \pi_s \\
		\pi_p & 0     & \pi_s & 0     & \pi_r \\
		\hline
		0     & 0     & 0     & 1     & 0     \\
		\hline
		0     & 0     & 0     & 0     & 1
	\end{bmatrix}
\]
